My work explores the intersection of urban analytics, generative design, and machine learning to understand how spatial systems and social dynamics co-evolve. Each project builds tools that bridge data and design, with a growing focus on generative urbanism and participatory spatial computing. I’m now consolidating my multidisciplinary experience into a cohesive research direction centered on how emerging technologies can help cities build resilience through cultural memory, adaptive expression, and human-centered design.
